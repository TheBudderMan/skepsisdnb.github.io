    A little about myself

    Tyler, AKA Formant, is a multi-talented creative force hailing from Winnipeg, Manitoba, Canada. With a diverse set of passions and skills. As a professional streamer, Tyler has cultivated an engaging online presence, captivating audiences with his infectious energy and authentic personality. Whether he's immersed in the latest video games or sharing his creative process, his streams provide a window into the dynamic life of a true creative genius. Beyond his gaming prowess, Tyler is an accomplished developer, leveraging his technical expertise to bring innovative ideas to life. His prowess in coding and software engineering allows him to push the boundaries of what's possible, constantly exploring new realms of digital creativity. Formant's artistic journey has amassed an impressive following, with over 3,500 YouTube subscribers and more than 85,000 views. His ability to connect with his audience, whether through his gaming streams, musical performances, or behind-the-scenes creative process, has earned him a devoted fanbase that eagerly awaits his next creative endeavor. As a multifaceted artist, Tyler, AKA Formant, is a true embodiment of the modern creative spirit. His boundless energy, technical proficiency, and artistic vision make him a force to be reckoned with in the ever evolving landscape of digital content and music production.
